Groups at RCCG Solution Ground

Small groups, community, and growth together.

Our various Groups

Media Group

The Media Group serve behind the scenes to make our services accessible and welcoming. We operate sound and PA mixing, camera and vision switching for livestreams, live projection and slides, stage lighting, video recording, editing, and social media publishing. Our team helps capture sermons, create on-screen graphics, and stream services to YouTube so people at home can join in.

Volunteers are trained — no previous experience required. Roles include sound desk, camera operator, livestream director, graphics/slide operator, and video editor.

Sundays

8:30 - 11:30 AM (set-up and service rota)


Choir Group

Our Choir lead worship and lift the congregation in song each Sunday and at special services. We sing a mix of contemporary worship, traditional hymns, and choral arrangements — aiming to serve God and encourage the congregation through heartfelt, well-prepared music.

Who can join: singers of all ages and abilities. We welcome sopranos, altos, tenors, basses, and anyone who wants to serve — no formal audition required for most roles. Musicians and accompanists are also encouraged to get involved.

If you can’t attend in person, you can still join our online gatherings and support outreach through prayer. We stream on YouTube and use Zoom for prayer meetings (Mon–Wed 7:00–8:00 PM; Sundays 8:30, 10:00, 11:30 AM).

Join Our Ushering Team

Our Ushers are the first warm face people meet on a Sunday. The team provides a welcoming, safe, and organized environment — greeting visitors, helping people find seats, assisting those with accessibility needs, distributing service sheets, and offering directions to children's and refreshment areas.

Roles include: greeter, door steward, welcome desk, and accessibility support. Ushers also help with collections, crowd flow, and ensuring children are safely guided to their groups.

  • Sundays — Serve during 8:30, 10:00 or 11:30 services (rota)

Evangelism Group

Our Evangelism team goes out into the community to share the message of hope and love. We engage with people through various outreach activities, events, and personal interactions — aiming to serve God and encourage others through our actions and words.

Who can join: anyone passionate about sharing the gospel and helping others. We welcome all ages and backgrounds, no prior experience required.

If you can’t participate in person, you can still support our outreach through prayer and online engagement. We stream on YouTube and use Zoom for prayer meetings (Mon–Wed 7:00–8:00 PM; Sundays 8:30, 10:00, 11:30 AM).
