Enoch Adejare Adeboye was born in 1942 into a humble family in Ifewara, Osun State, Nigeria. Raised in poverty—he often recalls not owning shoes until he was eighteen—he attributes his success to qualities such as focus, diligence, consistency, and determination. In 1967, he married Foluke Adenike Adeboye, and in 1973 he committed his life to Christ through the ministry of Josiah Olufemi Akindayomi, founder of the The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG). Ordained a pastor in 1977, Adeboye became known for his passion for evangelism, organizing Bible studies, crusades, revivals, and large evangelistic gatherings across southwestern Nigeria.

In 1981, following the death of Akindayomi, Adeboye became General Overseer of RCCG, a role that marked the end of his academic career and the beginning of a new phase of ministry expansion.

About the Continental Overseer Pastor Leke Sanusi, a Barrister and Solicitor successfully practiced law for over ten years before answering the call to pastoral ministry in 1997. His conversion story is likened to that of the biblical Apostle Paul in many ways. As a Pastor, he is passionate about seeing people set free from all manners of oppression and affliction of life. He is a contagious prayer warrior, speaking to thousands of audiences around the world; God, confirming his words with signs and wonders. Pastor Leke is Senior Pastor of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, Victory House, London, U.K. He is also the Continental Overseer of RCCG Europe Continent, serving more than two thousand churches, as well as Chairman of the RCCG Charity in the United Kingdom. A prolific writer, Pastor Leke has written and published more than thirty books including the annual Command the Year series. He is married to Pastor Bola and they are blessed with children.
