Youth Life & Events

Creative, active and faith-filled gatherings for 13–25s, monthly meetups, socials, worship nights and outreach. After being baptised in water, young people begin a journey with God: engaging in monthly meetups, summer outings, discipleship and service, growing together in faith, community and leadership.
