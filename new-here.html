Our Weekly Online Services

We host live online gatherings each week so you can join from home or wherever you are. Everyone is welcome — bring questions, prayer requests, or simply observe.

  • Monday — 7:00–8:00 PM
  • Tuesday — 7:00–8:00 PM (mainly prayer meetings)
  • Wednesday — 7:00–8:00 PM (Bible study)

On the first day of every month we hold a corporate fasting & prayer meeting on Zoom; this runs in addition to the Monday–Wednesday schedule. Check our contact page for Zoom and livestream links.

Join Our Evangelism Team

Everyone is invited to join our evangelism work. Our evangelism team meets every Saturday from 11:00 AM to 12:30 PM to go out into the local community — come along to share hope, pray with people, and support our outreach.

If you can’t attend in person, you can still join our online gatherings and support outreach through prayer. We stream on YouTube and use Zoom for prayer meetings (Mon–Wed 7:00–8:00 PM; Sundays 8:30, 10:00, 11:30 AM).

Got a Question?

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are services like?

Our services are vibrant and engaging, featuring contemporary worship, relevant teaching, and opportunities for community connection. Usually lasts about 180 minutes.

2. Where should I park?

We have ample parking available on-site for all attendees. Please follow the signs and parking attendants for guidance.

3. What should I wear?

We recommend dressing comfortably and modestly. There is no strict dress code, so feel free to wear what makes you feel at ease.

4. Where do my children go during the service?

We provide a safe and engaging environment for children during the service. Our children's ministry is staffed with trained volunteers who ensure a fun and educational experience for kids of all ages.
